Slow-Cooker Pulled Pork

Ingredients

  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3/4 cup ketchup
  • 3 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon mustard powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 (3 to 4 pound) pork shoulder, trimmed of excess fat
  • Salt and pepper
  • Buns and coleslaw for serving

Instructions

  1. Combine onion, ketchup, tomato paste, apple cider vinegar adn spices in the bowl of a slow cooker. Season pork shoulder all over with salt and pepper then add to slow cooker, covering it with ketchup mixture. Cover and cook on HIGH for 5 to 6 hours or on LOW for 8 to 10 hours until very tender (the mea should easily fall apart with a fork).
  2. Remove pork from slow cooker and transfer to bowl. Shred with two forks and toss with juices from the slow cooker. Serve on buns with coleslaw.
